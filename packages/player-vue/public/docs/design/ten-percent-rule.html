              OPTIMAL LEARNING

Signs You're in the Learning Zone

  • Mild frustration: "This is hard, but I can do it"
  • Occasional mistakes: ~20-30% error rate is healthy
  • Moments of flow: Fully engaged, time passing quickly
  • Satisfaction after success: "I got it!"

Signs You're NOT in the Learning Zone

Too Easy (Comfort Zone)

  • Mind wandering
  • Boredom
  • Going through motions
  • ~0-5% error rate

Too Hard (Panic Zone)

  • Anxiety
  • Wanting to quit
  • Feeling stupid
  • ~50%+ error rate

ğŸ˜¤ Frustration as Signal

Here's the counterintuitive insight: frustration is a positive signal.

From the Encouragements

"If you're feeling frustrated right now, that frustration is a huge sign of success... you're about to move forward."

The frustration of being in the 10% stretch zone means:

  • Your brain is working hard (neuroplasticity in action)
  • You're at the edge of your current capability
  • Growth is imminent
  • A breakthrough is coming

The Research

Studies show that learners who experience moderate difficulty perform better on retention tests than those who find learning easy. The struggle is the learning.

âš¡ Adaptive Difficulty

SSi continuously adapts to keep learners in the 10% zone:

Signals We Monitor

Signal Too Easy Just Right Too Hard
Response latency Very fast Moderate Very slow / timeout
Duration match Perfect Close Very long / cut off
Skip usage Frequent Occasional Never
Revisit usage Never Occasional Frequent

How We Adapt

If Too Easy

  • Shorten pause duration
  • Increase new LEGO introduction rate
  • Use longer/harder phrases
  • Accelerate spaced rep progression

If Too Hard

  • Extend pause duration
  • Slow new LEGO introduction
  • Use shorter/easier phrases
  • More review, less new content
  • Offer breakdown for M-type LEGOs

AdaptationEngine

The AdaptationEngine in @ssi/core monitors learner signals and adjusts difficulty in real-time:

  • Tracks rolling average latency
  • Detects "spikes" (sudden difficulty)
  • Adjusts pause multiplier
  • Triggers breakdown for struggling LEGOs

Key Parameters

Parameter Default Description
pause_multiplier_base 2.0 Default pause = 2x target audio
pause_multiplier_min 1.5 Minimum for advanced learners
pause_multiplier_max 3.0 Maximum for struggling learners
spike_threshold 1.5 Latency ratio triggering spike
