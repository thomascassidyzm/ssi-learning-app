# Journey Background Image Prompts

## Technical Spec (prepend to every prompt)

> **768 x 1376 pixels, portrait orientation. Monochrome sumi-e ink wash style on off-white/cream paper. No colour — grey tones only, ranging from pale washes to near-black ink. The image will be displayed at 18% opacity with `mix-blend-mode: multiply` on a warm mist-coloured background (#F5F0EB), so detail should be bold enough to read at low opacity but not so heavy it muddies. Leave the top ~20% of the image as mostly empty sky/paper (UI text overlays here). The main composition occupies the centre and lower two-thirds. Output as WebP, target ~100-150KB.**

## Consistent Story Elements

Every image tells the same story: **a journey of learning**. Two small figures at the bottom of a winding path, heading upward through a culturally evocative landscape toward a landmark destination at the top. The path should wind naturally (not a straight line) — switchbacks, curves, bridges — creating visual depth and a sense of progression.

- **Two figures** near the bottom — a learner and a guide, small but visible, walking together. Simple silhouettes, not detailed faces. One slightly ahead of the other.
- **A winding path** from bottom to top — stairs, road, trail, canal towpath — whatever fits the culture. The path is the spine of the composition.
- **A destination** near the top — a culturally significant building or landmark. The journey's goal.
- **Cultural landscape** surrounding the path — vegetation, architecture, terrain, animals — all specific to the culture, rendered in ink wash layers with atmospheric perspective (foreground bold, distance pale/misty).
